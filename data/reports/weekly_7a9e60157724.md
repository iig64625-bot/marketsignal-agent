## Key Changes by Competitor
### Coze
- **[product]** ByteDance open-sourced the coze repository under Apache 2.0, accepting community contributions. _(confidence: medium)_
  - Analysis: Open-sourcing is a community-building and developer-mindshare move, not necessarily a revenue move, but it pressures closed competitors on transparency.
- **[pricing]** Coze moved to a per-seat model that resembles the classic SaaS playbook and signals confidence in team expansion. _(confidence: high)_
  - Analysis: Per-seat pricing improves land-and-expand dynamics and is easier for procurement teams to evaluate than usage-based alternatives.
- **[hiring]** Coze is hiring a VP of Engineering for its agent platform, indicating a step-change in platform investment. _(confidence: medium)_
  - Analysis: VP-level hire typically precedes a major roadmap acceleration and signals ByteDance's continued commitment to Coze outside China.
- **[product]** Coze is investing heavily in workflow automation, matching where enterprise buyers want to consolidate tooling. _(confidence: high)_
  - Analysis: Workflow Templates lowers the activation bar for non-technical builders, expanding the addressable market beyond the existing developer base.
- **[gtm]** Coze launched native CRM connectors (Salesforce / HubSpot / Zoho), enabling direct integration into existing revenue stacks. _(confidence: high)_
  - Analysis: This collapses the time-to-value for sales and RevOps buyers and shortens Coze's evaluation cycle in mid-market accounts.
### FastGPT
- **[risk]** FastGPT has not yet published SOC2 documentation, which blocks deals with regulated buyers. _(confidence: high)_
  - Analysis: This is a short-term weakness but a near-term disqualifier for healthcare, financial services and EU public-sector prospects.
- **[product]** FastGPT added multi-modal RAG, an area Dify has not yet addressed with first-class support. _(confidence: high)_
  - Analysis: Multi-modal retrieval is increasingly requested by enterprise buyers with image- and audio-heavy knowledge bases; the gap may show up in competitive deals.
- **[risk]** Users report degraded accuracy on FastGPT above ~60k context tokens. _(confidence: medium)_
  - Analysis: Long-context accuracy regressions create churn risk in accounts with large knowledge bases and shift buyer attention to retrieval quality instead of model size.
- **[product]** FastGPT 4.7 ships a visual DAG editor, narrowing the gap with Coze's workflow templates. _(confidence: high)_
  - Analysis: Visual DAG editors dramatically improve builder productivity; both Coze and FastGPT now have stronger no-code offerings than Dify.
